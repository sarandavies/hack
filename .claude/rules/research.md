# Research rules
- Multi-motion discovery (8 motions); competitor customers are one source, never the only one.
- Two-pass funnel: ≤50 cheap triage → 12-15 deep. Adaptive loop: typed actions only, ≤6 (fast)/12 (thorough) per candidate, always addressing the highest-value evidence gap; stop on qualified/rejected/budget/insufficient-evidence.
- Record structured decision summaries, never private chain-of-thought.
- Signals ranked commercially (tier 5 buying > tier 2 complaint). Action/budget/ownership/timing beats emotion.
