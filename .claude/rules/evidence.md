# Evidence rules
- Claims ↔ evidence is many-to-many (claim_evidence); first-party status lives on claim_evidence, relative to the claim.
- Verbatim excerpt check, known-source-id allowlist, snippet rejection, staleness check for current-status claims, syndication dedup by content hash.
- Failed claims: reject or weaken, one targeted repair max, never silently upgrade partial support.
- Speculation only in labelled research notes; never affects qualification.
